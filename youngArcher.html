Who is the young archer?

The Young Archer was born with a congenital growth-hormone condition that gave him the outward appearance and voice of an adolescent boy, hence the name The Young Archer. He was actually not fond of that name at all as he was often the eldest person in the room, but was treated as if he was still a child. Sucks doesn't it?
